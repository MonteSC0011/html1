Washington Huskies football

The Washington Huskies football team represents the University of Washington in college football. Washington competes in the NCAA Division I Football Bowl Subdivision (FBS) as a member of the North Division of the Pac-12 Conference. Husky Stadium, located on campus, has served as the home field for Washington since 1920.
